GenAI Coding Problem
AI Leadership Insight & Decision Agent

Overview
Your organization wants to build an AI-powered assistant for leadership that can:
1. Answer questions about the current status and performance of the company
using internal documents.
2. Evolve into an autonomous research and decision-making system capable of
handling open-ended strategic questions.

Task 1 – AI Leadership Insight Agent (Simpler)
Objective
Build an AI agent that can understand company documents and answer leadership
questions about the organization’s current working, performance, and status.

Inputs
 A folder of company documents (e.g.:
o Annual reports
o Quarterly reports
o Strategy notes
o Operational updates)
 A natural-language question from leadership
Example Questions
 “What is our current revenue trend?”
 “Which departments are underperforming?”
 “What were the key risks highlighted in the last quarter?”

Functional Requirements
The agent should:
1. Ingest and process company documents
2. Retrieve relevant information for a given question
3. Generate a concise, factual answer grounded in the documents

Constraints
 Use Python
 Any LLM / embedding approach may be used
